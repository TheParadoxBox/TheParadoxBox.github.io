The Dump: An assortment of resources

Welcome to the Dump, the place on this site where I put things that are useful for me to have on the Internet. It's a separate part of the website for a few reasons:

  1. It's messy
  2. It's unrelated to the main content of the site
  3. It's nothing that needs to be on the front page

Subjects are as follows:

  • FRC 2023: A simplified version of the FIRST Robotics Competition 2023: Charged Up manual. Includes a cheatsheet from Team 4481: Rembrandts.
  • Sofa Savvy: A fake digital storefront created for an Economics project. Downloads Baloo Tamma 2 and Lato fonts from Google Fonts.

Content on subpages may be adapted from other sources.
