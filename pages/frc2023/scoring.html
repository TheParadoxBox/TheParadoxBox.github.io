Scoring

The scoring for Charged Up is as follows. Numbers are in Match Points, except in the Ranking Points category. Huge thanks to Team 8046: The Lakerbots.

Autonomous

Robot bumper leaves its Community - 3
Game Piece on low Node - 3
Game Piece on middle Node - 4
Game Piece on high Node - 6
Docked on Charge Station, not engaged - 8
Docked on Charge Station, engaged - 12

Note 1: only the points from one robot being docked and/or engaged are counted in Auto. If, for example, two robots are docked and engaged, only 12 points will be awarded.

Note 2: a robot cannot start on the Charge Station.

Teleoperated

Game Piece on low Node - 2
Game Piece on middle Node - 3
Game Piece on high Node - 5
Link (three Game Pieces in a row) - 5

Note: four Game Pieces in a row is not two Links. If you had all the Game Pieces in one row, you would need six pieces for two Links.

Endgame

Robot completely inside Community - 2
Docked on Charging Station, not engaged - 6
Docked on Charging Station, engaged - 10

Ranking Points

Win - 2
Tie - 1
Lose - 0
Sustainability Bonus (Five Links) - 1
Coopertition Bonus (three Game Pieces in both Co-op Grids) - Sustainability Bonus requirement is down to four Links
Activation Bonus (26 points from Charge Station) - 1
